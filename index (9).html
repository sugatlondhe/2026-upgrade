Why upgrade instead of replacing the machine?

Short answer: SP 40 series weight sorters are highly reliable, and SP 60 series machines are mechanically identical except for the all-new control unit—so upgrading targets the control layer without replacing proven mechanical hardware.

Data integrity controls

With the new control unit, your SP 40 series gains audit trail, domain authentication options, and secure storage/transmission of data—designed to support regulated data integrity expectations (21 CFR Part 11 / EU GMP Annex 11).

Enhanced connectivity and control

The new control unit integrates with your domain controller for user management and data access. It can also share data with SCADA devices using OPC/UA.

Reduced resource costs

With tighter security and streamlined data transfer, operating procedures around your SP 40 series can be simplified—from day-to-day operations to accessing batch reports and data.

What capabilities do you gain with the new control unit?

Short answer: FLEX2 on modern hardware, plus the Nexa data suite for extracting, sharing, and integrating data sources into operations.

Future-proofed control and data access

The new control unit for your SP 40 series machines runs our latest operating system, FLEX2. This runs on the latest hardware, rigorously tested and underpinned by certified supply. It will continue to be updated as the business and regulatory landscape evolves.

It also includes the Nexa data suite, which provides standard functionality and extra options for extracting, sharing and integrating data sources into your operations.

Upgrade and validation

All SP 40 series upgrades are carried out on-site by experienced engineers, using a tried and tested process:

  1. Quotation for upgrading one or more machines, including IQ as standard plus options for OQ and PQ.
  2. On-site upgrade and IQ, plus OQ if selected.
  3. Support for PQ with documentation, test scripts and hands-on support, as agreed.
  4. Full documentation, traceability information and certification.

Support as-new

Once upgraded, you are entitled to the same level of support as if it were a new machine.

We commit to maintaining our customers' SP 40 series weight sorters for at least seven years following their upgrade.

Any change parts you hold for SP 40 series machines continue to function exactly as before and remain compatible with SP 60 series weight sorters and accessories.
